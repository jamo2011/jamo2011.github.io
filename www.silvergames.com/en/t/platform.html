Platform games, also known as platformers, are a genre of video games characterized by their emphasis on precise jumping and platform-based challenges. These games typically feature a side-scrolling perspective and require players to navigate their character through a series of platforms, obstacles, and enemies to reach the end of each level.

In our platform games, players control a character that can run, jump, and sometimes perform additional actions like attacking or using special abilities. The main objective is to progress through levels by maneuvering the character across platforms, avoiding hazards, and defeating enemies. Timing and precision are essential as players must carefully time their jumps, overcome obstacles, and collect power-ups or items along the way.¬†Platform games often feature a variety of themed levels with distinct visual designs and unique gameplay mechanics. Players may encounter challenging puzzles, hidden areas, and boss battles that add depth and complexity to the gameplay.
