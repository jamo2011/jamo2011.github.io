Duck Hunt

Games ¬ª Shooting Games ¬ª Hunting Games

🦆 Duck Hunt is a free hunting game where you shoot ducks which your dog startles. One of the classic, formative game experiences of the digital era has been brandishing the Zapper and shooting pixelated ducks as they fly across the screen. This online duck hunting game is a fan remake based on the popular Nintendo game of the same name. You get to relive the challenge of chasing after those zig-zagging ducks as well as the frustration of the giggling dog, when you manage to miss all of them.

Make your way through the many seasons of the game and shoot fowl from the sky. Bask in the glory of being a grand, or sometimes not so grand, digital hunter. Enjoy Duck Hunt and give those airborne quacks something to quack about. Play online and for free on Silvergames.com!

Controls: Mouse = Aim/Shoot, R = Reload
