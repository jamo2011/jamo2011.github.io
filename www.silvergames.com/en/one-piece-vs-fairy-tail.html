One Piece vs Fairy Tail

Games » Action Games » Fighting Games

One Piece vs Fairy Tail is a fascinating arcade fighting game for 2 players in which you can play as the characters from the popular anime One Piece. You can play this game online and for free at Silvergames.com. Luffy D. Monkey is one of the best-known characters in the history of animated television series. After eating a devil fruit he obtained supernatural powers that make him practically invincible. But today you can fight with any of the other skilled One Piece characters as well.

Choose Zoro, Sanji, Nami or play as one of their powerful enemies like the clownish Buggy, Doflamingo or Sir Crocodile. This great combat game with retro style graphics will allow you to use the special powers of all the characters. Throw a shower of punches like the lovable protagonist or swing Zoro's 3 swords to defeat your opponents. You can play in arcade mode, versus mode or 2 player mode to defeat your friends in classic fights. Have fun playing One Piece vs Fairy Tail!

Controls: Player 1: WASD = move / block, UIOKL = attack. Player 2: Arrows = move / block, 12345 = attack
